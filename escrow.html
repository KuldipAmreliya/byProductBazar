How Escrow Works

Our escrow service ensures safe and secure transactions between buyers and sellers in the waste trading industry.

1

Agreement & Payment

Buyer and seller agree on terms, and buyer deposits payment into our secure escrow account.

2

Verification & Delivery

Seller ships the waste material and provides proof of delivery. We verify the transaction.

3

Release & Completion

Once buyer confirms satisfaction, we release payment to seller and complete the transaction.
