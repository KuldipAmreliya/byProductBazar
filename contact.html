Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform

How do I start selling waste on your platform?

Simply register for an account, complete your profile, and create a listing for your waste material. Our team will review and approve your listing within 24 hours.

What types of waste materials can I trade?

We support various industrial waste categories including metal scrap, plastic waste, chemical waste, electronic waste, textile waste, and more. All materials must comply with environmental regulations.

How do you ensure secure transactions?

We use escrow services, verify all users, and provide secure payment processing. All transactions are protected and monitored for your safety.

Do you provide transportation services?

Yes, we partner with verified logistics providers to offer transportation services. You can arrange pickup and delivery directly through our platform.
